Our Mission Statement

We strive to foster a collaborative, transparent, and accountable environment where team members feel confident in doing their best and communicating openly. Our mission is to uphold the Scrum values: commitment, courage, focus, openness, and respect, so that we can deliver high-quality results while continuously supporting each other’s growth and success.

Our Rules

If a team member falls behind, they should communicate this during meetings or immediately when the issue arises. The team can then reassess task priorities, redistribute the workload if necessary, and break down tasks into smaller manageable pieces. If a team member is not contributing or is uncooperative, we can address the issue respectfully in a private conversation. If the issue persists, bring it up with the professor. In terms of conflict resolution, any disagreements will be handled professionally through open discussion, focusing on the best outcome for the project rather than personal opinions.
